NMAP tutorial:

nmap -sn 10.0.0.* (Find all devices on a network)
nmap -sn 10.0.0.0/24 (Find all devices on a network)
------------------------------------------------------
arp-scan -l (Find all devices on a network)
--------------------------------------------------------
nmap 10.0.0.4 (on a machine)
-------------------------------
nmap -sV 10.0.0.4 (version of ports)
--------------------------------
nmap -sV -p21,22 10.0.0.4 (specifying ports 21 and 22)
----------------------------------------------------------
nmap -sV -p- 10.0.0.4 (all available ports)
-------------------------------------------------------------
nmap -sV -Pn (-Pn to specify no ping for websites forbidding ping requests)
-----------------------------------------------------------
nmap -sS 10.0.0.4 (service)
----------------------------------------------------------
nmap -O 10.0.0.4 (operating system)
---------------------------------------------------------
nmap -A -p21 10.0.0.4 (All scan) (targeting port 21//if not we can remove -p21)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
nmap -A -p- 10.0.0.4 (All scan All ports)
--------------------------------------------------------
nmap -A -p21 -oN /root/Desktop/nmap-port21-scan.txt 10.0.0.4 (save the results to a txt file)
